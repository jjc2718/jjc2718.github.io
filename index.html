Hi! I am a graduate student in the Bioinformatics and Computational Biology research group at Tufts University. My advisor is Prof. Lenore Cowen.

My research interests are fairly broad. My current work primarily focuses on biological networks; to be more specific, we are studying disease-gene associations in the human PPI network. Other interests of mine include algorithms and machine learning, as applied to biological problems.

When I'm not doing the grad school thing, I enjoy playing and watching most sports, cooking, reading, lifting weights, consuming far too much tea, and having philosophical discussions with my debugging duck.
